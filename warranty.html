Why You Should Consider a Pre-Owned Car

A PO used car is a Pre-Owned car and they are very different from the average used car. A car can be certified either by a dealer using an automakers specifications, or dealers can certify cars on their own. There are a number of advantages that come along with certification:

A multipoint inspection: An automaker or dealer will check on hundreds of parts and systems to ensure they meet the minimum requirements.

An extended warranty: Typically, the preowned car will get a warranty that extends the miles covered, the months remaining, or both to the bumper-to-bumper warranty or powertrain warranty.

Other incentives: Preowned cars often come with a vehicle history report at no extra charge, roadside assistance programs and more.

One element to weigh, though, is that Preowned cars typically run more expensive than non-Preowned cars, so consider the benefits versus that extra cost and decide which approach is best for you.
