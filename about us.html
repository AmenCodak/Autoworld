AUTOWORLD

Since incorporation in 1987 Autoworld Motors; our flagship company, has been driven by the vision of remaining timeless in relevance and value. By constantly keeping to our Brand promise, Autoworld Motors has become a household name providing the Nigerian people with vehicles from a robustly diversified auto dealership, comprising of five luxury and two value brands. These brands are showcased in our ultra modern showrooms nationwide.

Autoworld Motors has followed through by making significant investments in state of the art Aftersales facilities across the country and training of specialized personnel to ensure that our customers stay satisfied while putting their vehicles on the road. In addition, through strategic partnerships Autoworld Motors ensures that wherever you are, our services are readily accessible.

Asides from the brands that Autoworld Motors represents, the company has also made it its duty to ensure that motorist are kept safe on the road and their vehicles in good condition by providing genuine automotive spare parts and automotive related products to the general public via distributors. Autoworld Motors sales and service locations are spread across the five geopolitical zones including Abuja, Kano, Gombe, Lagos, Ekiti, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Uyo, Calabar, Enugu to mention a few.

With a view to give back to our society, Coscharis regularly engages in social responsibility investments especially through the sponsorship of both public and private human development activities such as the annual week of caring, Public Private Auto Training Programmes at our auto training center amongst others.
